What Happens Next

1
Your application is reviewed by a civil registration officer within 3–5 working days.
2
You will receive an SMS and/or email confirmation with your application status.
3
Visit any Civil Registration Office with your reference number and original documents for verification.
4
Upon approval, your National ID Card will be issued within 10–15 working days.
